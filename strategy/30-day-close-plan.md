## The Logic

No one hires a stranger. The sequence is always:

```
They see you → They check you out → They trust you → They have a problem → They reach out → You close
```

You can't control when someone has a problem. But you can control how many people see you, and what they find when they check you out. In 30 days, we need to:

1. Make your digital presence bulletproof (so when someone Googles you, they're impressed)
2. Get visible in Mérida's business owner circles (so the right people know you exist)
3. Activate warm leads (people who already know you but haven't bought)
4. Create one undeniable case study (Dr. Ramírez → proof for every future client)
